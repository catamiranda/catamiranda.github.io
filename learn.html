    Academic Trajectory

    My research agenda focuses on gender issues, citizenship education, political socialization, educational inequality, and early childhood education. My teaching experience focuses on data analysis and rights-centered education. In addition to consultancies at national and international levels, I coordinate studies and collaborate in academic divulgation networks.
